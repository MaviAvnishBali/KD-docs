Privacy Policy

Kila Darbar · Last updated: 8 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Kila Darbar ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the Kila Darbar mobile application (the "App"). By using the App, you agree to the practices described here.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account details: your name, phone number, and email address, provided when you sign in or complete your profile.
  • Delivery information: the delivery addresses you save (street, area, landmark, city, state, pincode).
  • Order information: items you order, order history, and booking enquiries (e.g. party hall / table reservations).
  • Device & notification data: a push-notification token so we can send you order updates and offers.
  • Authentication data: we use Google Firebase Authentication to verify your phone number (OTP) or Google sign-in.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account and authenticate you.
  • To process, prepare, and deliver your orders and bookings.
  • To send order status updates and, where permitted, offers and promotions.
  • To provide customer support and improve the App.

3. How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal data. We share information only as needed to run the service — for example with delivery personnel to fulfil your order, and with trusted service providers such as Google Firebase (authentication and notifications). We may disclose information if required by law.

4. Data Retention & Account Deletion

You can delete your account at any time from within the App: go to Profile → Delete Account. This permanently removes your account and associated personal data (profile, saved addresses, order history, and loyalty points).

You may also request deletion by emailing us at kiladarbarfo0d@gmail.com. We may retain limited records where required by law (for example, transaction records for tax/accounting) for the period required.

5. Data Security

We use industry-standard measures, including encrypted connections (HTTPS) and access controls, to protect your information. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, but we work to safeguard your data.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them.

7. Your Rights

You may access or update your profile details in the App, or contact us to request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
